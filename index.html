    Frequently Asked Questions

    Frequently Asked Questions About MAAC Raipur

    What is the eligibility criteria to join MAAC Raipur?

    The eligibility criteria to join MAAC is Class 12 pass. To know about MAAC Raipur courses and for free career counselling, call on our toll-free number 1-800-209-6222 or visit your nearest MAAC Raipur centre.

    What benefits do students get from MAAC Raipur?.

    MAAC Raipur lays strong emphasis on providing the best infrastructure to train students. Career Development Training (CDT), industry visits, course-specific workshops, seminars, educational events and webinars related to jobs are conducted to keep students updated with the changing graph of the animation, VFX, and filmmaking industry. It also provides placement assistance to eligible students on successfully completing their course.

    What are the courses available at MAAC Raipur?

    MAAC Raipur offers a wide range of career-oriented courses in 3D Animation, Visual Effects, Game Designing, AR/VR, Broadcast, and Multimedia and Design along with Short Term Courses. For details, check our courses section, or locate your nearest MAAC Raipur centre here.

    What are the fees for MAAC Raipur courses?

    MAAC Raipur offers a wide range of career-oriented short-term & career courses. The duration of each course is different & the timings are flexible to fit the requirements of students. From time to time, MAAC also offers scholarships and discounts to deserving students. Keeping in mind various factors, fees for MAAC courses have been kept variable. For the latest fee details, get in touch with your nearest MAAC centre.

    Does MAAC Raipur has a loan or any other easy payment methods to pay course fees?

    Yes, MAAC Raipur offers easy payment methods including EMI options and financial assistance programs to help students pay their course fees. For more information, contact MAAC Raipur centre.

    What are the placements and career prospects available after doing a MAAC Raipur course?

    MAAC Raipur provides Career Development Training to prepare students for the job market. Throughout their course, students are encouraged to attend lectures by industry experts, and workshops and events to develop their job skills.
